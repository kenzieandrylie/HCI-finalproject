Our Story

LeTflix was founded in 2021, LeTflix is an entertainment website that provides a collection of the latest films, LeTflix itself is inspired by the problems that exist and are often experienced by movie fans, often people are confused in determining what movies they want to buy on DVD or download movies through the website, but often this method causes new problems such as a scratched DVD will damage the movie on the DVD, besides that we can also download our movies through many website platforms that provide it but also often they don't provide complete episodes, subtitles , there are ads, poor resolution, requires storage to save the downloaded movie. With this LeTflix, we provide solutions to these existing problems so that movie fans can enjoy movies without the occurrence of an obstacle that can interfere.
